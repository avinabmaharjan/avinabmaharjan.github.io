REBUILDING FROM ZERO

Trace of a Quiet Climb.
[ Builder · Learner · Still Becoming ]

I wasn't born talented. I wasn’t the smart kid or the disciplined one. My life has been a series of detours—restarts, quiet failures, and vanishings.

Now, I choose to surface. This site isn’t a portfolio of mastery; it is a log of my attempt to emerge from the void. From ashes, slowly.

01 // THE NARRATIVE

My story isn't linear.

BACKGROUND

I used to run from hard things. I procrastinated, avoided responsibility, and let fear dictate my timeline. I watched others move ahead while I fought invisible battles that don’t make it to Instagram. The truth is less heroic: I was stuck.

CURRENT REALITY

I decided to stop vanishing. Now, I treat every restart as a rebirth. I study, I break code, I build scripts that crash, and then I fix them. I’m leaving breadcrumbs here to find my way back. This is my anchor.
